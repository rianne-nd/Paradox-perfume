How can we help?

Find answers to your questions about our products, shipping, and more.

search
Products from what country? add_circle
Our fragrance oils are sourced directly from Grasse, France, the perfume capital of the world. This ensures top-tier quality and longevity. The blending, aging, and bottling process takes place here in Manila to ensure freshness.
What are your concentrations? add_circle
All our perfumes are Eau de Parfum (EDP) with a guaranteed 30% oil concentration. This is higher than the standard 15-20% found in many commercial scents, ensuring a long-lasting fragrance that stays with you for 8-12 hours.
Do you accept returns or exchanges? add_circle
For hygiene reasons, we do not accept returns on opened bottles. However, if your bottle arrives broken or damaged, please send us a photo within 24 hours of delivery, and we will happily send a replacement free of charge.
How long is shipping? add_circle
Standard shipping within Metro Manila takes 2-3 business days. Provincial shipping takes 5-7 business days. We ship via our trusted courier partners to ensure your package arrives safely.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help. Get in touch with us for any further inquiries.
